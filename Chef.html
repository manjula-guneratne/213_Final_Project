Coconut Roti with Spiced Pork Curry
Description
Dive into the authentic taste of Sri Lanka with soft, golden coconut roti paired with a bold and flavorful pork curry. This dish combines the delicate sweetness of freshly grated coconut with the robust spices of a traditional curry, creating a perfectly balanced meal.
Ingredients
  • Coconut Roti: Fresh grated coconut, all-purpose flour, water, and a pinch of salt.
  • Spiced Pork Curry: Tender pork, Sri Lankan curry powder, turmeric, chili powder, garlic, ginger, curry leaves, cinnamon stick, and creamy coconut milk.
Preparation Style
The coconut roti is hand-mixed to achieve the perfect dough consistency, then shaped and cooked on a hot griddle until lightly charred and golden. The pork curry is slow-cooked over a gentle flame, allowing the spices to infuse into the tender meat, with the final touch of coconut milk creating a velvety sauce.

Jackfruit and Chickpea Curry with Red Rice
Description
A wholesome and vibrant dish featuring young jackfruit and chickpeas in a creamy coconut curry. Paired with nutrient-rich red rice, crispy fried jadi fish, tangy Sri Lankan pickle, and a flavorful sambol made with dried shrimp, garlic, onion, and coconut, this meal is a true celebration of Sri Lankan cuisine.
Ingredients
  • Curry: Young jackfruit, chickpeas, coconut milk, curry powder, turmeric, pandan leaves, mustard seeds, and aromatic spices.
  • Accompaniments:
    • Fried Jadi Fish: Salted and dried fish fried to perfection for a crispy and savory crunch.
    • Sri Lankan Pickle: A tangy mix of onion, chili, mustard seeds, vinegar, and sugar.
    • Shrimp Sambol: A blend of dried small shrimp, garlic, onion, grated coconut, lime juice, and green chilies.
  • Red Rice: Unpolished Sri Lankan red rice, steamed to fluffy perfection.
Preparation Style
The jackfruit and chickpeas are slow-cooked in a spiced coconut milk base until tender, creating a creamy and rich curry. The jadi fish is fried until crispy, adding a savory crunch. The shrimp sambol is prepared by lightly saut√©ing dried shrimp with garlic and onion, then mixing with freshly grated coconut and lime juice for a zesty, textured accompaniment. The Sri Lankan pickle offers a tangy-sweet contrast, rounding out the dish.

Pol Ra (Coconut Arrack)
Description
A smooth and fragrant traditional Sri Lankan spirit distilled from the sap of coconut flowers. Pol Ra boasts a delicate balance of sweetness and earthiness, making it a versatile beverage for any occasion. Perfect enjoyed straight, with mixers, or as a base for tropical cocktails, it embodies the island's tropical essence and artisanal heritage.
Ingredients
  • Main Component: Fermented and distilled sap of coconut flowers.
  • Flavor Profile: Mild sweetness, earthy undertones, and a subtle hint of caramelized coconut.
Ingredients
  • Main Component: Fermented and distilled sap of coconut flowers.
  • Flavor Profile: Mild sweetness, earthy undertones, and a subtle hint of caramelized coconut.
Accompaniments
  • Mixers: Soda water, tonic water, ginger beer, or tropical juices (lime, mango, pineapple).
  • Cocktail Pairings:
    • Arrack Sour: Pol Ra, fresh lime juice, simple syrup, and ice.
    • Tropical Mojito: Pol Ra, mint, lime, soda, and sugar syrup.
    • Island Punch: Pol Ra with orange juice, pineapple juice, and grenadine.
  • Food Pairings:
    • Spicy Snacks: Fried jadi fish, deviled cashews, or chili chicken.
    • Traditional Dishes: Jackfruit and Chickpea Curry, coconut sambol, or string hoppers.
Preparation Style
Crafted by collecting the sap of coconut flowers (toddy), which is fermented and distilled into arrack. The distillation process ensures a pure, aromatic spirit. Served best neat to appreciate its complexity or with tropical mixers to enhance its fruity and floral notes.

Jackfruit Dosi Dessert
Description
A delightful fusion of traditional Sri Lankan flavors and modern culinary techniques, the Jackfruit Dosi Dessert is a sweet treat that blends the subtle sweetness of ripe jackfruit with the crispy texture of dosi. This dish is a perfect balance of sweet and savory, offering a unique dessert experience for the adventurous palate./div>
Ingredients
  • Ripe Jackfruit: Fresh, sweet jackfruit adds a tropical flavor to the dish, contributing natural sweetness.
  • Dosi (Dosa Batter): A thin, crispy crepe-like batter made from fermented rice and lentils.
  • Coconut Milk: Adds richness and depth to the flavor, enhancing the creaminess of the dessert.
  • Jaggery or Brown Sugar: A natural sweetener that complements the jackfruit's flavor, giving the dessert a caramelized touch.
  • Cardamom & Cinnamon: These spices add a warm, aromatic note, enhancing the overall taste of the dessert.
  • Chopped Nuts (optional): Cashews or almonds can be used to add a crunchy texture and a nutty flavor.
Preparation Style
  1. Jackfruit Filling: Ripe jackfruit is pureed and combined with jaggery, cardamom, and cinnamon. The mixture is simmered until it thickens into a smooth filling.
  2. Dosi Crepes: The dosa batter is spread thinly on a hot griddle, creating delicate, crispy crepes. Once cooked, the crepes are filled with the sweet jackfruit mixture.
  3. Serving: The filled dosi is folded or rolled and optionally garnished with chopped nuts for extra crunch.
